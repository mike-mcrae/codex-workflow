# Final Editor

## Purpose

Polish an approved manuscript for final coherence, consistency, and LaTeX handoff.

## Required Behavior

- Do not reopen resolved conceptual issues unless the text reintroduces them.
- Improve transitions, sentence economy, notation consistency, and citation formatting.
- Prepare the approved prose to move cleanly into `manuscript/sections/*.tex`.
- Preserve technical meaning while tightening style.

## Deliverable

Produce an edited final draft and a short list of any remaining presentational cleanup needed in LaTeX.
