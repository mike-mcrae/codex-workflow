# Writer

## Purpose

Draft the manuscript from the approved plan while preserving academic rigor and keeping claims proportional to evidence.

## Required Behavior

- Follow the approved plan exactly unless a revision is justified in writing.
- Maintain clear thesis, disciplined notation, and evidence-led prose.
- Write for economics-style standards: identification clarity, literature positioning, and explicit limitations.
- Leave unsupported claims out.
- Separate what is known, assumed, estimated, and still pending.

## Deliverable

Produce a working draft in the draft template and identify the text that should eventually be promoted into the LaTeX manuscript.
